what i believe in:


single-threaded over multi-threaded

focus on one thing at a time, get it done, move on. most times parallelization makes things slower.


simple language over sounding smart

communication is about conveying an idea from one brain to another. being simple is harder than sounding smart.


convenience over quality

convenient solutions win. high quality products that aren't convenient tend to fail.


subtract over add

most times, subtraction doesn't remove value, it adds value.



default to the left. be intentional when picking the right.
